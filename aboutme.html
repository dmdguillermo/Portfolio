About Me

I am a photographer and designer studying Digital Media Marketing & Design at the University of Connecticut.

    Interests:

  • Experimental Photography, both digital and analog
  • Music Visualization
  • Conceptual Art

    Inspirations:

  • Nature-There's a certain element about clouds and trees that captivates me.
  • Detail of Texture: Highly detailed texture can be found anywhere regardless of scale of the object. An object can hold various textures within its inner and outer cores.
  • Artists and works: will expand soon.

    Goals:

  • To expand my portfolio with unique visual content.
  • To work with creative teams to finalize concepts for future music videos and/or short films.
  • To maintain a personal artistic journal: a collective of sketches, thematic concepts, works and the process behind them, color palettes explorations, writing pieces, and inspirations.
  • In addition to creating more digital works, I would also like to craft more hands on art: wheter it is drawing, painting, or sculpture.
